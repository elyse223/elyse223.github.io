Description and Explanation:

In Formula 1, there are 10 teams with 2 drivers each. The teams are McLaren, Ferrari, Mercedes, Red Bull Racing, Williams, Aston Martin, Racing Bulls, Kick Sauber, Haas, and Alpine. In the next Formula 1 Season, the 2026-2027 season, there is going to be a 21st team, Cadillac. The drivers for this team are to be Valteri Bottas and Sergio Perez. Both drivers drove in Formula 1 in previous years, but lost their seat for the 2025-2026 season.
