Driver's Championship

With the 2025 season coming to a close, Lando Norris became the World Champion in Abu Dhabi on December 7. He won by only 2 points from Max Verstappen, who ended up getting second in the Championship. The final race was a constant fight between three drivers who were extremely close in the points. One DNF or safety care could have changed the whole outcome. Lando Norris, Max Verstappen, and Oscar Piastri were so close in points that it came down to the Grand Prix in Abu Dhabi.

The driver qualified with Max Verstappen at pole, Lando Norris in second, and Oscar Piastri in third. At the very beginning of the race, Max Verstappen kept his lead, and Lando Norris was alomst immedietely passed by Oscar Piastri, putting the Championship at risk right off the bat. Lando Norris needed to get thrird place to win the Championship, anything lower and he would come in second or even third. The rest of the race was very constant and throughout all the pit stops the top three staying in the same positions. During the end of the race, Lando Norris was under a lot of pressure from Charles Leclerc, and Oscar Piastri started to catch up to Max Verstappen. The finishing order was the same as what they started in.
